Project Overview

About This Project

Background on the Gahanga Management System and the two parties involved.

Saint Joseph Gahanga Parish

A Catholic parish in Gahanga, Rwanda. Currently managing all parishioner records — households, sacraments, finances, and group memberships — on paper. The goal is to digitize all of this into a single, locally hosted management system.

AbyTech Hub

The technology company hired by the parish to design and build the Gahanga Smart Parish Management System. Responsible for all technical specification, development, and delivery of the 8-module software platform.

What Is Being Built

A custom web application covering 8 modules: Christian Management, Fund Management, Publications, Group Management, Sacraments, Parish Website, Reports & Dashboard, and External Integrations (SMS, MOMO, social media).

Data Privacy Principle

All parishioner data will be stored on a local server at the parish premises — not on any foreign cloud platform. This was confirmed as a non-negotiable requirement by the parish leadership at the first meeting.
