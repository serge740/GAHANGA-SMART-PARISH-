Why This Meeting Was Called

Meeting Objective

"To exchange ideas on the data we need to put into the software that will assist in the digitization of the parish."

The purpose of this meeting was specifically to agree on what information the software must capture for each of the 8 modules — not to discuss technology, cost, or timeline. Those are separate discussions.

What Was Agreed

Decisions Made

Two decisions were recorded in the meeting minutes.

1

Data requirements for the software were agreed upon

The committee reviewed and agreed on all the data that must be collected and stored in the software in order to build it. The full data specification covering all 8 modules is documented in this report (see Modules section below).

2

Mr. KARANGWA Joseph to submit a list of technology experts (coders) to Padiri

Mr. Joseph committed to providing Padiri with a list of people with technical expertise who could help build the software — to determine whether they would contribute voluntarily or for pay.

Software Data Requirements

All 8 Modules

The following 8 modules were reviewed at this meeting. For each module, the committee agreed on exactly what data the software must capture and what actions it must support.

Module 1

Christian Management

Registration, editing, updating, transferring, viewing, and deactivating household records.

Module 2

Fund Management

All categories of parish financial collections — Mass offerings, subscriptions, donations, and more.

Module 3

Publications

Announcements, committee reports, strategic plans, action plans, and upcoming events.

Module 4

Group Management

Creating and managing all parish groups — priests, committees, chapel zones, and Catholic Action.

Module 5

Sacrament Management

Tracking all 6 sacraments per person — with class attendance and exam records where applicable.

Module 6

Parish Website

Public-facing website for the parish. Requirements to be provided by Mr. KARANGWA Joseph.

Module 7

Statistical Reports

Dashboard showing totals and breakdowns — by gender, sacrament, marital status, education level.

Module 8

Integrations

SMS gateway, digital payments (MOMO/bank), local government, other churches, social media.

Module 1

Christian Management

This module manages all household records. The data entry form is based on the existing paper household registration form.

Module 1 · Use Cases
Actions the software must support
  • Registration — Record a new household using the existing household registration form
  • Edit Urugo — Correct or change existing household information
  • Update Urugo — Update household records when family changes occur
  • Transfer Household — Move a household from one chapel zone to another
  • View Urugo Details — View the full record of any household
  • Deactivate — Mark a household as inactive (e.g., family has left the parish)
Module 2

Fund Management

This module tracks all money collected by the parish. The following categories of collection were agreed upon at this meeting.

Module 2 · Collection Categories
Types of parish financial collection to be tracked
  • General Church Collection — General offering collected at Mass
  • Mass Offering — Offering given at Mass
  • Amasekuru — Alms / tithe collection
  • Mass Intention Requests — Booking a Mass for a special intention
  • Special Celebrations — Collections for special occasions: funeral (uwitabye Imana), wedding (uwashyingiwe), baptism (uwabatirishije), and others
  • Subscriptions / Membership Contributions
  • Donations — Gifts and donations to the parish
  • Miscellaneous — Any other financial collection not listed above
Module 3

Publications

This module handles all documents and announcements that the parish publishes or communicates internally and publicly.

Module 3 · Content Types
What can be uploaded or published through the software
  • Ibirangwa (Amatangazo) — Announcements and notices
  • Reports (Raporo) — Quarterly reports of each committee, minutes of general meetings, annual reports
  • Igenamigambi ry'Icyerekezo — Strategic plan (Plan stratégique)
  • Gahunda y'Ibikorwa by'Umwaka — Annual action plan (Plan d'action) — both per committee and for the whole parish
  • Upcoming Events (Gahunda ziteganyijwe) — Future scheduled activities and events
  • Parish Programme — The general weekly and monthly parish schedule
Module 4

Parish Group Management

This module manages all groups and organizations within the parish. It covers creating groups, recording members, and tracking each member's role.

Groups to be Created

  • Abasaseridoti — Priests
  • Inama nkuru — General Council
  • Ncungamutungo / Iterambere — Finance & Development Committee
  • Komisiyo — Commissions
  • Chapel Zones
  • Impuza — Sub-zone groupings
  • Family Cluster Groups
  • Catholic Action Groups

Member Data to Capture

  • Name (Izina)
  • Personal details (Amakuru bwite)
  • Position / Role within the group

Module 7

Statistical Reports & Dashboard

This module provides a data dashboard showing the total count of Christians in the parish and breakdowns across multiple dimensions.

Dashboard Breakdowns by Category

  • Gender — Female, Male, Children, Youth
  • Sacrament status — which sacraments each person has received
  • Education level
  • Marital status (see right column)

Marital Status Categories

  • Ababana bombi — Both spouses living together
  • Abapfakazi — Widows / Widowers
  • Ababyariye murugo — Single parents
  • Abatandukanye — Separated / Divorced
  • Abongeye gushaka — Remarried

Sacrament Breakdown Dimensions

The dashboard must be able to show counts per sacrament:

Baptism Eucharist Confirmation Marriage Anointing of the Sick Holy Orders
Module 8

Integrations

The software must be able to connect with and exchange data with external systems and platforms.

Module 8 · External Connections
Systems the software must integrate with
  • SMS Gateway — Send text messages to parishioners directly from the system
  • Digital Payment — Accept offerings and subscriptions via mobile money (MTN MOMO) and bank
  • Local Government (Inzego za Leta) — Data exchange with Rwanda government agencies
  • Other Religious Organizations — Interoperability with other churches or religious bodies
  • X (Twitter) — Post and share parish content to X
  • WhatsApp — Send parish communications via WhatsApp
  • Facebook — Share parish content and announcements on Facebook
